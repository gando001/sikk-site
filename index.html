Jumping Jeff Will you help?

Jeff is very hungry but all of the bigger monkeys will not share their fruit. Jeff cannot reach the fruit above so he got a pogo stick to jump with but needs your help to collect the fruit. Test your skills in this fun and easy game. Swipe to make Jeff jump and tilt your device to move him. Go to the monkey bank to buy more fruit and upgrades for the next level.
